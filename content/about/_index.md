# Our Story

s3lim is designed to help engineers and DevOps teams regain control of their S3 storage costs. By providing high-performance inventory analysis and clear metrics, we make it easy to uncover hidden costs, identify duplicate objects, and optimize storage classes without the overhead of heavy, complex tools.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to bring transparency and efficiency to object storage. We believe that understanding your storage should be fast, secure, and straightforward.

## Open Source First

s3lim is built with an open-core philosophy. Our core analysis engine is open source, allowing you to run it anywhere and verify exactly how your data is handled. We are committed to maintaining a robust, community-friendly tool while providing enterprise-grade deployments for teams that need them.

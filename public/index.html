How it Works

Deep Prefix Visibility

Recursive prefix aggregation reveals exactly where your data is growing. Identify 'hot' prefixes and candidates for Intelligent Tiering or Lifecycle policies with surgical precision.

Actionable Cost Reduction

Surface hidden storage costs. s3lim detects incomplete multipart uploads and non-current versions that traditional tools miss, enabling immediate savings.
